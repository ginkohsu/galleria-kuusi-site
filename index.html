OPEN CALL

Mid April 2026 – Mid October 2026

Galleria Kuu-si is an artist & curator-run exhibition space in central Helsinki. A compact street-facing gallery for experimental and intimate presentations.

We invite artists to activate the space as a small art laboratory in the city. Exhibitions run for two weeks.

Fee: €400 per exhibition (shared for group shows).
Artists manage installation, insurance, and invigilation coordination.

Send proposal + images to:
info@galleriakuusi.com

ABOUT

Galleria Kuu-si is a small independent artist-run exhibition space near Kamppi, Helsinki. Focused on new work, dialogue, and intimate formats.
