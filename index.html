Open Call

Mid April 2026 – Mid October 2026

Galleria Kuu-si is an artist & curator-run, non-profit exhibition space in central Helsinki. Our compact street-facing space invites experimental, intimate, and process-based work.

We are looking for artists to activate the space as a small art laboratory in the city. Exhibitions run for two weeks.

Fee: €400 per exhibition (shared if group show).
Artists handle installation, insurance, and invigilation coordination.

Send proposal + images to:
info@galleriakuusi.com

About

Galleria Kuu-si is a small independent exhibition space located near Kamppi, Helsinki. Run by artists, the gallery focuses on intimate presentations, new work, and experimental formats.

The space operates as a flexible platform for emerging and mid-career artists, with an emphasis on dialogue, process, and visibility within the city.

Address: Helsinki, Finland
