OPEN CALL ABOUT EXHIBITIONS

We are not interested in exhibitions that rely on long explanations to function.

Repetition is safer than failure, and therefore less interesting.

We look at the work first, not the CV.

We respond to artists, even when the answer is no.

We do not charge application fees.

We are bored by Western contemporary art’s default narratives.

We don’t believe revolt needs to be loud, large, or monumental.

We are suspicious of the word “museum-grade.”

We value specificity over scale, intensity over polish, and risk over coherence.

If a project could exist anywhere, it probably doesn’t belong here.

OPEN CALL

MID APRIL – MID OCTOBER 2026

Dear Artist,

Galleria Kuu-si is an artist- and curator-run, non-profit exhibition space in central Helsinki, just 500 meters from Kamppi Metro Station. Our compact 22.5 m² space has a street-facing window on Eerikinkatu, perfect for sharing your work with the public.

We’re looking to create an art lab in the city — a place for fun, bold, diverse, and experimental projects. We provide basic supervision during open hours, help document and promote your show on social media, and offer curatorial support if needed. We do not take commissions on artwork sales.

Artists are responsible for installation, de-installation, transport, insurance, and hosting the exhibition opening. We are happy to assist on site within our abilities.

A two-week slot from mid-April to mid-October 2026 costs 450 euros, covering operational costs, including space rental, basic supervision, and social media promotion/documentation.

HOW TO APPLY

Submit ONE PDF in English (or Finnish if needed) to info@galleriakuusi.com including:

  • Page 1: Briefly tell us about yourself (one paragraph) and your preferred time period.
  • Page 2: A short description of your exhibition idea and medium (max one page).
  • Optional Page 3: Condensed CV (max two pages).
    • Page 4: Portfolio.

Deadline: 23:59, March 8th, 2026

Selected artists will be notified before April 1st. We are a very small team, so we can’t reply to every application individually.

Also note that our next round of call is in June/July 2026 :)

Warmly,
The Kuu-si Team

ABOUT

Galleria Kuu-si is an artist-run exhibition space located at Eerikinkatu 33, Helsinki.

The name of our space, Kuu-si, is inspired by W. Somerset Maugham's The Moon and Sixpence. It reflects the challenge many artists face: pursuing creative dreams while navigating the practical realities of everyday life.

Our aim is to support the art community in building a sustainable art practice. We are seeking funding to make the space rent-free whenever possible. We also encourage artists to collaborate with our twin sister, the craft and artisan shop Honobono, located next door. This collaboration can include workshops or consignment of smaller art/design objects, helping artists create a more sustainable practice.
